We are envisioning Sustainable agriculture that plays a vital role in improving food security while conserving land resources. BIWODA is adopting methods that nurture the soil and enhance biodiversity; rural communities can sustain their livelihoods and protect the land for future generations.

In the hilly Biringo community traditional cooking there extensive use of firewood and leads to deforestation. Deforestation disrupts biodiversity, accelerates soil erosion, and reduces the land's fertility.

BIWODA seeks support to enable the Shift from traditional cooking methods to energy-efficient solutions helps to conserve land by reducing deforestation.
