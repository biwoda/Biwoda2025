Rural women are adopting innovative cooking technologies to reduce reliance on firewood and preserve land resources. Innovations:-Energy-Efficient Stoves: Using less firewood, reducing the pressure on forests. Briquettes from Natural Vegetation like wetlands- Currently we are experimenting the anaerobic digestion technology by a State University of New York at Buffalo student named Ashley. This innovation will carbonize the organic matter like papyrus reeds. Biringo women want to create fuel briquettes from renewable resources like papyrus reeds, which are abundant and sustainable in Biringo.
